Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

Your Privacy Matters! At StyloOutfit, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our Service:

Type of Information Examples Purpose
Account Information Name, email address, password Account creation and authentication
Profile Information Body measurements, style preferences, gender Personalized outfit recommendations
Photos Profile pictures, body photos for avatar 3D avatar creation and virtual try-on
Communication Messages, feedback, support requests Customer service and improvements

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use StyloOutfit, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Session data, preferences, analytics

1.3 Information from Third Parties

If you sign in using Google or other third-party services, we receive:

  • Name and email address
  • Profile picture (if public)
  • Basic profile information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Provide and Improve Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate personalized outfit recommendations
  • Create your 3D avatar for virtual try-on
  • Process your requests and preferences
  • Improve our AI algorithms and features
  • Develop new features and services

2.2 Communication

  • Send account-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send updates about new features (with your consent)
  • Provide customer service

2.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Verify user identity
  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions

2.4 Analytics and Research

  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Measure effectiveness of features
  • Conduct research to improve AI models
  • Generate aggregated, anonymized statistics

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to legal requests
  • Protect our rights and property

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our Service:

  • Cloud Hosting: AWS, Google Cloud, or similar (for data storage)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (for usage analysis)
  • Email Services: SendGrid, Mailgun (for transactional emails)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal (if we introduce paid features)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government or regulatory requests
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

3.3 Business Transfers

If StyloOutfit is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on need-to-know basis
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing and multi-factor authentication
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Backups: Regular encrypted backups for data recovery

4.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your password secure and confidential
  • Logging out of your account on shared devices
  • Notifying us immediately of any unauthorized access
  • Using strong, unique passwords

4.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you promptly as required by applicable law.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Know what data we have about you

5.2 Correction and Update

You can:

  • Update your profile information anytime
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Complete incomplete information

5.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your:

  • Account and associated data
  • Specific pieces of information

Note: Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

5.4 Opt-Out

You can opt-out of:

  • Marketing emails (unsubscribe link in emails)
  • Analytics cookies (browser settings)
  • Data collection for certain purposes

5.5 Object and Restrict

You can:

  • Object to processing of your data
  • Restrict how we use your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

5.6 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@stylooutfit.in

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

6.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our Service.

6.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic functionality (login, security) Session/Persistent
Analytics Cookies Understand how you use our Service (Google Analytics) Up to 2 years
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences Up to 1 year
Functional Cookies Enable enhanced features and personalization Persistent

6.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (block or delete cookies)
  • Opt-out tools (e.g., Google Analytics opt-out)
  • Privacy settings on our website

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

7. Data Retention

7.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our Service to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our AI models and algorithms

7.2 Retention Periods

Data Type Retention Period
Account Information Until account deletion + 30 days
Photos and Avatars Until deletion or account closure
Usage Data Up to 2 years (anonymized after 1 year)
Communication Records Up to 3 years for customer service
Legal/Compliance Data As required by law (typically 7 years)

7.3 Deletion Process

When you delete your account:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics
  • Backups are purged within 90 days
  • Legal retention requirements are honored

Users aged 13-18 should have parental consent before using our Service.
